AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The main objective of our project is to help the people by using scientific knowledge and to find some ways that can be helpful
to reduce and prevent the impact of human activities on climate. We have mainly focused on some innovative ideas
including turbine over big chimneys to produce electricity , balloon containing hydrogen in it, producing electricity from
the sound energy and many other things.Our ideas once applied will be useful to people.

We hope that our ideas will be guided by experienced juries and it will be made more applicable by contribution of child scientists
and this is possible only if we will get select for the next level.
